About Bella vista

Our story

Since 1910, Bella Vista has been a cherished name in Italian dining, rooted in tradition and family values. What began as a small family trattoria in Italy has now blossomed into a destination for authentic cuisine in the heart of the city. For over a century, we have stayed true to our philosophy using the finest ingredients, preserving timeless recipes, and serving every guest with warmth. Each dish at Bella Vista is a tribute to our heritage, blending old-world flavors with contemporary elegance. From our first wood fired oven to today’s modern kitchen, our passion for Italian food remains unchanged.
